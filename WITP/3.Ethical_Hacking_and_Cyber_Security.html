ETHICAL HACKING AND CYBERSECURITY

About

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and programs from digital attacks. These cyberattacks are usually aimed at accessing, changing, or destroying sensitive information; extorting money from users; or interrupting normal business processes.

What Will You Learn

Ethical Hacking teaches individuals offensive skills for identifying vulnerabilities, while CyberSecurity teaches defensive tactics for preventing unauthorized access, data breaches, and cyber threats. In a linked digital ecosystem, this protects data integrity and system security.
